# Mechanical Design

R3Bin was designed as a vertically organized physical prototype with distinct functional levels.

## Structure

- Approximate outer dimensions: 500 mm x 400 mm x 800 mm.
- Main panels: 5 mm foamed PVC.
- Rear panel: transparent acrylic for process visibility.
- Assembly: screws, nuts, and printed brackets/supports.

## Functional Levels

- **Level 0:** user interface, inlet opening, automatic lid.
- **Level 1:** photo booth and image-capture region.
- **Level 2:** rotating disk, NEMA 17 motor, guide/funnel path.
- **Level 3:** removable bucket with six manually divided compartments.

## Final Bucket Design

The final physical bucket uses manually cut 30 pt styrene sheets to create six internal compartments. Earlier ForgeCAD designs included printed divider hubs, rails, and bridge structures, but those were not used in the final physical prototype because they were less simple and required more printed material.

This design decision reduced PETG usage, simplified assembly, and made the bucket easier to maintain.
